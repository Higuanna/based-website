WHAT IS BASEDPAD?

Basedpad is a dynamic community of brazen pioneers within the TAP/Ordinals space where innovation, collaboration, and cutting-edge technology converge. Built on the foundation of creativity and shared purpose, Basedpad brings together builders, artists, intellectuals, entrepreneurs, and investors to drive positive change within the Bitcoin ecosystem and beyond.

BUILT ON TAP

Born in early 2023, Basedpad are pioneers in the Bitcoin metaprotocols ecosystem, including the TAP/Ordinals space. TAP is an OrdFi-enabling protocol for Bitcoin Ordinals. It works entirely without the use of L2 chains but utilizes tapping - a simple mechanism to verify transactions within the protocol.
